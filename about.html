Our Story

A Hidden Gem That Became a Landmark

Tucked on B.B Road, right opposite Kothanoor Gate in Vapasandra, Shreyas Family Restaurant started as a simple dream — serve honest, delicious food at prices everyone can afford.

Today, we're the most talked-about dining spot near SJCIT. Students from the college walk in for a warm lunch between lectures. Families come on weekends to celebrate. Working professionals stop by for quick, satisfying dinners. Everyone leaves full and happy.

Our kitchen runs on fresh produce, hand-ground spices, and a lot of love. No frozen shortcuts, no compromises — just the kind of food that keeps people coming back.
